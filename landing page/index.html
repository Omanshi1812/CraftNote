Notebooks

Notebooks Available at our shop
Spiral Notebook

Spiral notebooks feature a spiral binder that holds the pages together. Spiral binding comes in a variety of sizes and thicknesses, including spiral wires and heavier plastic spirals.

Bound Notebook

Bound notebooks have a flat spine. T hey are held together with sewed pages that are bonded and coated. These pages can be lined, blank, grid, dotted, or any other combination.

Scientific notebook

They are intended for use by students, scientists, and laboratory personnel. Each page has areas for additional information such as the notebook's page number, title, project number, dates, and signatures.
